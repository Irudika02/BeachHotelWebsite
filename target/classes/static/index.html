Common Questions

Find Your Answers

What is the check-in and check-out time?
Check-in time is from 2:00 PM, and check-out time is until 12:00 PM. Early check-in or late check-out is subject to availability.
Does the hotel provide airport transfers?
Yes, we provide luxury airport transfers from Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA). Please contact us with your flight details to arrange a pickup.
Are there any vegetarian/vegan options in the menu?
Absolutely! Our menu features a wide variety of authentic Sri Lankan vegetarian and vegan dishes prepared with fresh local ingredients.
Is the resort kid-friendly?
Yes, we are very family-oriented. Our Family Villa is perfect for kids, and we offer a range of activities and kids' menus.
